- John, Bob and Alfred betting on a dog race;
- John starts with $50, Bob with $75 and Alfred with $45;
- Before the race starts, they decide if and how much to bet;
- They can change their minds before the race starts;
- Minimum bet: $5, maximum bet: $15;
- Just an unique bet by person by race;
- Double or nothing: whe winner doubles the money bet or loses what he has bet;
- Four dogs runs randomly;
- A 4 GreyHound object matrix and a 3 Guy object matrix;
